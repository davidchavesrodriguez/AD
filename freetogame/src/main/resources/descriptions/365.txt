Naruto Online is a free-to-play MMO based on the popular anime series and manga, developed by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game follows the story line of the anime series with five new characters that represent the elements of earth, water, fire, wind, and lightning. Players choose one of these characters to play as.

Players have the opportunity to relive key moments from the series while building their own ninja team consisting of characters from the show. The game features a combo fighting system that takes advantage of the team option as well as a variety of modes from single player to multi-player dungeons. There are also giant world bosses to fight and several ways for players to earn XP without getting bored.

Naruto is a broswer-based game and can be played without the need of any downloads.
